> **Location Info:** Shows the name and era (AC) of the town or dungeon. Optional locations are shown outside the main flow arrows.

* **💬 [Skit]:** Which one is superior? (どっちが上か)

### 🌿 [Sub-Event] 01: Dio's Flower (P.097)
> **Side Quest:** Events occurring at this timing. Numbers correspond to the list starting on page 97.

### 🟢 2. Go out to the World Map

---

## 📍 [AC. 4408] Town: Valhalla Town (P.103)

### 💬 [Chat]
* `Condition Required` **I've been looking for you forever** (ずっと探してた)
> **Note:** Blue text in the guide indicates chats that require specific conditions to trigger.

### 🟢 3. Enter the Item Shop
> After receiving a lecture on shopping, you will obtain the **Collector's Book**, **Monster Book**, and **Battle Memo**. Initially, they will all be mostly empty.

* **💬 [Chat]:** Etos is everyone's mother (エトスはみんなのお母さん)

### 🟢 4. Return to the town entrance and battle with the **Vokuvua** (ボコボア)

---

## 📍 [AC. 4408] Town: Olive Village (P.104)
> **Tip:** This is the only town at this stage where you can buy items, weapons, and food. Purchase supplies here so you don't struggle in the upcoming **Tower of Sparkle**.

---

## 📍 [AC. 4408] Dungeon: Tower of Sparkle (P.118)

### 💬 [Chat]
* Mermel isn't bad, the bad one is... (メルメルは悪くない、悪いのは…)
* That masked guy (あの仮面のヤツ)
* What kind of creature is Kruhl? (クルールってなんて生物)

> **Note on Chats:** Chats are divided into those triggered by story progress and those triggered by conditions like leveling up. This flowchart only lists story-triggered chats. Check **P.239** for battle-related chat conditions.

### 🟢 5. Examine the right switch on the 1st floor to proceed
> **Dungeon Gimmick:** Dungeons contain various gimmicks, including switches. If you see something suspicious, examine it.

### 🟢 6. Proceed to the 3rd floor and battle with the **Vokuvua** (ボコボア)
